For the pod to track events in FigShare, the pod app must be registered with FigShare. After registration, the Client ID and Client Secret that FigShare assigns must be saved in the pod. The following steps will help you through the process of registering the Pod with FigShare:

1. Register your pod with FigShare

Please visit the FigShare Settings Page and click on Create Application. The information required to fill out the form is provided below:

  • Application URL : {{ url_for("index_page.on_index", _external=True) }}
  • Callback URL: : {{ url_for("oauth2_portal.get_authorization", portal_name="figshare", _external=True)}}

2. Save Client ID and Client Secret

Upon successful registration of the app, you should see a page which shows what the Consumer ID and Consumer Secret are. Please copy that information and paste it in the fields Client ID and Client Secret in the form below and hit "Save".
