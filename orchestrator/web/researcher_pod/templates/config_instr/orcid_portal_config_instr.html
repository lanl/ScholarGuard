For the pod to track events in ORCiD, the pod app must be registered with ORCiD. After registration, the Client ID and Client Secret that ORCiD assigns must be saved in the pod. The following steps will help you through the process of registering the Pod with ORCiD:

1. Register your pod with ORCiD

Please visit the ORCiD Developer Page and register your Pod as an ORCiD App. The information required to fill out the App registration form is provided below:

  • Homepage URL: : {{ url_for("index_page.on_index", _external=True) }}
  • Authorization Callback URL : {{ url_for("oauth2_portal.get_authorization", portal_name="orcid", _external=True)}}

2. Save Client ID and Client Secret

Upon successful registration of the app, you should see a page which shows what the Client ID and Client Secret are. Please copy that information and paste it in the corresponding fields in the form below and hit "Save".
